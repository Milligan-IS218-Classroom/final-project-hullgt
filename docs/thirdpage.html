Who AM I?

My name is Graydon Hull, I am a 20 year old student and follower of Christ. I am a collegiate athlete attending Milligan University in East Tennessee. I run Cross Country and Track, my favorite of which is Cross Country. I was born in Johnson City, TN and have lived in the area my entire life. I study Computer Science at Milligan. When not in School I live with my Parents and my only sibling, my older brother. I love the outdoors, my hobbies include: running, fishing, shooting, and hiking. I enjoy almost anything outdoors. I have worked at Food City Grocery in Gray, TN for the past 2 years. I am about to start my senior year credit-wise at Milligan and am excited to hopefully enter into the information technology sector soon.
